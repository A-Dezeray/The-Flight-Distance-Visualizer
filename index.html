Why Wingspan Matters

Lift Generation: Larger wingspans create more surface area, generating greater lift force that keeps the aircraft aloft longer.

Lift-to-Drag Ratio: Longer wings typically have better aerodynamic efficiency, reducing induced drag and allowing the aircraft to glide farther.

Glide Performance: With increased lift and reduced drag, aircraft with larger wingspans can maintain altitude longer and travel greater distances.
